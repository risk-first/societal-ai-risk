# Societal AI Risks

Threats that emerge as AI systems become increasingly powerful and integrated into society. These risks concern **AI's impact on civilisation** â€” affecting democracy, human agency, economic structures, and global stability.

## Actor Taxonomy

Risks in this framework involve different types of actors:

| Actor | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **AI System** | Internal | The AI itself exhibiting unexpected, misaligned, or harmful behaviors |
| **Malicious Actors** | External | State actors, criminals, or bad actors exploiting AI for harm |
| **Systemic Forces** | Systemic | Market pressures, platform incentives, and economic dynamics that enable risk |
| **Governments & Institutions** | Systemic | Policy decisions and regulatory failures that amplify risk |
